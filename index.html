10Project Tool Selection

Primary Tool & Rationale

The three project management tools I chose to explore are Smartsheet, Asana, and Monday.com; the one I really enjoyed using‚Äîand will continue to use‚Äîis Monday.com. That was, coincidentally, a management tool a lot of the interviewees I spoke to talked about using. I can see why Monday would be a good project management tool to use, not just for predictive, but also for hybrid and agile.

I look forward to getting as familiar as possible with this project. This one was more intuitive to use and had an easy upload option for files I already have saved to my computer. It is free to use, beginner-friendly, and very intuitive in adding new views and dashboards to configure it to your liking. The one that sold me was the mobile option that Asana and Smartsheet didn't allow, not to mention Smartsheet only allowed me to use whatever the "organization" had previously created. This was one of the key reasons why I also stayed away from Smartsheet, and Asana was a little too plain for my liking.
